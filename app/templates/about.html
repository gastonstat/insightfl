About this project

Every morning millions of women and men apply from 12 to 20 personal care products to themselves, according to the Cosmetic, Toiletry and Fragrance Association. Most of us take for granted that the products we use don't contain ingredients with toxic substances. Unfortunately, this is far from true.

Unlike pharmaceuticals, cosmetics and personal care products are not tested for safety before they go into the market. Pharmaceuticals have to go through strict premarket approval before they can be sold and used. Cosmetics on the other hand are only regulated post market, and do not have to be tested and proven safe before they are sold.

The onus is on the consumer to select personal care products that don't contain harmful chemical ingredients. So the problem is: How do you know that the products you use are safe?

skincareprods.info helps you score your personal care products so you can make a more informed decision about using your products.
